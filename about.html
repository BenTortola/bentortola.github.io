My career goal is to become an animator specializing in traditional 2D animation while also developing skills in 3D animation. Although I currently have limited experience, I have learned foundational concepts through high school courses and online tutorials. My long-term goal is to refine my skills through practice and eventually work professionally in animation, particularly in the entertainment industry such as shows, films, and or gaming. The main reason I am drawn to animation is its power as a storytelling tool. Unlike books, which rely entirely on imagination, or live-action productions, which are limited by physical constraints, animation allows creators to build limitless worlds. Whether it is a fantasy setting, a sci-fi adventure with vibrant color palettes, or a reimagining of real-life perspectives, animation offers endless creative possibilities. I am also deeply interested in the visual aspect of animation. I have always enjoyed drawing and sketching in my spare time and learning how drawings can be brought to life through motion greatly inspired me.
